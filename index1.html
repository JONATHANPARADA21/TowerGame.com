HOW TO PLAY?

Hi everyone, welcome to the TOWER CHALLENGE can you be able to put the pyramid into the next box? Rules are simples, you only can move one piece at time however you can not move one if there is a piece over the one you want to move, also you can not to put a piece over one that is smaller that you want to move, i hope you enjoy this game. First you have to touch the piece you want to move then touch the box you want get that one; you could start touching the piece number 1 then touch the box number 2.
